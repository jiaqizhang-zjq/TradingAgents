**核心功能**:

- ✅ **性能追踪**: `@log_execution_time` 装饰器

- ✅ **依赖注入集成**: 通过Container管理logger单例

**日志配置**:
```python

LOG_FILE = "tradingagents.log"
MAX_BYTES = 10MB
BACKUP_COUNT = 5
FORMAT = "%(asctime)s - %(name)s - %(levelname)s - %(message)s"
```
